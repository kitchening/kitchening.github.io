Now Available!

Our Gluten Free Peanut Butter Cookies are available for purchase at Edible Canada on Granville Island, in the bakery department at Urban Fare Yaletown and at Fresh Street Farms in Surrey.

Our peanut butter cookies are handcrafted locally in the Lower Mainland using all natural ingredients. Our peanut butter contains... peanuts. No margarine, hydrogenated oils or any other silliness. Just chewy, peanut-y goodness.

Ingredients

Peanut butter (peanuts), brown sugar, gluten-free flour blend (corn starch, white rice flour, brown rice flour, milk powder, tapioca flour, potato starch, xanthan gum), butter, sugar, eggs, milk, vanilla (vanilla extract, water, alcohol, sugar), baking soda, salt.
