Our Ginger Chocolate Cookies

Our ginger chocolate cookies are handcrafted locally in the Lower Mainland using all natural ingredients. No margarine, hydrodgenated oils or any other silliness. We use real butter, callebaut chocolate, freshly grated ginger and fancy molasses to make the best ginger chocolate cookie we hope you've ever had.
