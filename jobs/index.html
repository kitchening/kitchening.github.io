Product Demonstrator / Sales

Description

We’re looking for a few awesome, outgoing individuals who are passionate about food (and hopefully passionate about macarons) to join us doing sales and product demos at various retail locations and high end grocery stores in Vancouver.

We like to work with people who are thinking about the long-term, and not just a job for the next few months. We’d love for you to stick around and get to know us, and we’d like to get to know you.

Schedule

Typically, we find the best time for product demos are usually:

  • Friday, Saturday or Sunday from either 12pm -4pm or 2pm-6pm
  • Occasional weekdays from 11am-3pm

Location

Typically the Vancouver downtown core, or other areas in Vancouver proper.

Responsibilities
  • Interacting with potential customers while providing macaron samples at various retail locations, primarily in or around the downtown core of Vancouver.
  • Setting up demo equipment prior to the demo.
  • Taking down and cleaning demo equipment after a demo.
  • A vehicle will be required to transport the various supplies necessary for the product sampling and demos.
  • Suitable freezer space will be required to store macaron samples prior to demo (room in a fridge freezer, or in a small deep freeze).
About You

You have a positive attitude and like to work with people who have the same. You’re a great salesperson, friendly, outgoing and enjoy both learning and educating people about new things.

Compensation

Hourly, commensurate with experience, but more importantly will be tied to suitability to the position and performance.

How To Apply

Send an email to Daniel (email hidden; JavaScript is required) with the title of this page in the subject line, and include the following:

  • Resume (bonus points if you skip the resume and just tell me what your experience is, and why you’re awesome for this position)
  • A little bit about yourself (a few sentences)
  • Your first experience with Kitchening macarons (if you’re familiar with them)
  • A few sentences about your favourite food in Vancouver (restaurants, bakeries, whatever!).
