Our peanut butter cookies are handcrafted locally in the Lower Mainland using all natural ingredients. Our peanut butter contains... peanuts. No margarine, hydrodgenated oils or any other silliness. Just chewy, peanut-y goodness.

Ingredients

Peanut butter (peanuts), brown sugar, flour, butter, sugar, eggs, milk, vanilla (vanilla extract, water, alcohol, sugar), baking soda, salt.
