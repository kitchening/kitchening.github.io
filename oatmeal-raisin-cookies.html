Now Available!

Our Oatmeal Raisin Cookies are available for purchase at all Urban Fare locations.

Our oatmeal raisin cookies are handcrafted locally in the Lower Mainland using all natural ingredients. No margarine, hydrogenated oils or any other silliness. We use real butter, chewy sultana raisins and delicious rolled oats to make what we hope is the best oatmeal raisin cookie you've ever had.

Ingredients

Brown sugar, rolled oats, flour, butter, raisins, eggs, baking soda, salt, natural vanilla extract, cinnamon.
