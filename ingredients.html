Salted Caramel Macarons

Ingredients: Almond flour, icing sugar (sugar, corn starch), egg whites, water, butter, sugar, cream, salt, natural colour (caramelized sugar).

Toasted Coconut Macarons

Ingredients: Almond flour, icing sugar (sugar, corn starch), sugar, egg whites, butter, milk, water, egg yolk, corn starch, shredded coconut, natural flavour extract (water, ethyl alcohol, natural flavour).

Chocolate Macarons

Ingredients: Almond flour, icing sugar (sugar, corn starch), sugar, egg whites, butter, milk, water, cocoa powder, egg yolk, corn starch, cocoa nibs.

Raspberry Macarons

Ingredients: Almond flour, icing sugar (sugar, corn starch), sugar, egg whites, butter, milk, water, egg yolk, corn starch, natural flavour extract (water, ethyl alcohol, natural flavour), natural colour (beet).

Pistachio Macarons

Almond flour, icing sugar (sugar, corn starch), sugar, egg whites, butter, milk, water, egg yolk, corn starch, natural flavour powder (dextrose, natural flavours), natural flavour extract (water, ethyl alcohol, natural flavour), natural colour (vegetable and plant extracts).

Vanilla Lavender Macarons

Ingredients: Almond flour, icing sugar (sugar, corn starch), sugar, egg whites, butter, milk, water, egg yolk, corn starch, vanilla (vanilla bean extractives, water, alcohol, sugar), vanilla beans, lavender.

Lemon Macarons

Ingredients: Almond flour, icing sugar (sugar, corn starch), sugar, egg whites, butter, milk, water, egg yolk, corn starch, fresh squeezed lemon juice, ascorbic acid, natural colour (turmeric).

Espresso w/Dark Chocolate Ganache Macarons

Ingredients: Almond flour, icing sugar (sugar, corn starch), sugar, egg whites, chocolate (unsweetened chocolate, sugar, cocoa butter, soy lecithin, natural vanilla flavour), cream, water, coffee, butter, vanilla (vanilla bean extractives, water, alcohol, sugar).

Mandarin Strawberry Macarons

Ingredients: Almond flour, icing sugar (sugar, corn starch), sugar, egg whites, butter, milk, water, white chocolate (sugar, cocoa butter, whole milk powder, soy lecithin, vanilla extract), egg yolk, corn starch, natural flavour powder (dextrose, natural flavours), natural flavour extract (water, ethyl alcohol, natural flavour), natural colour (vegetable and plant extracts).
