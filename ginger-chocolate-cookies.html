Now Available!

Our Gluten Free Ginger Chocolate Cookies are available for purchase at Edible Canada on Granville Island, in the bakery department at Urban Fare Yaletown, Giovane Cafe (on Burrard) and at Fresh Street Farms in Surrey.

Our ginger chocolate cookies are handcrafted locally in the Lower Mainland using all natural ingredients. No margarine, hydrogenated oils or any other silliness. We use real butter, callebaut chocolate, freshly grated ginger and fancy molasses to make the best ginger chocolate cookie we hope you've ever had.

Ingredients

Gluten-free flour blend (corn starch, white rice flour, brown rice flour, milk powder, tapioca flour, potato starch, xanthan gum), chocolate (unsweetened chocolate, sugar, cocoa butter, soy lecithin, vanilla), molasses, butter, brown sugar, freshly grated ginger, cocoa, spices, baking soda.
