Our Macarons

Our macarons are handcrafted locally in the Lower Mainland using all natural colours and flavours. We are always working on improving our craft, and our desire is to create for you the best macarons you’ve ever had. But it’s always better when you can use the words of customers.

Ingredients

Almond flour, icing sugar (sugar, corn starch), egg whites, butter, milk, sugar, eggs, water, corn starch, natural flavor powders (dextrose, natural flavors), natural flavor extracts (water, ethyl alcohol, natural flavor), natural colors (including beet, turmeric, annatto, hibiscus, vegetable and plant extracts, tapioca dextrose, caramelized sugar).

Some flavours may also contain: chocolate (unsweetened chocolate, sugar, cocoa butter, soy lecithin, natural vanilla flavor), cream, coconut, lavender, coffee, tea, cocoa powder, lemon zest, vanilla (vanilla bean extractives, water, alcohol, sugar), vanilla beans, herbs, spices, salt.

Macaron Favours
